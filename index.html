Motivation

Source of Stability

My personal need for a logical framework for life that also feels right has pushed me into a corner: I cannot accept a doctrine that misses one of those two points, and there is no such framework out there that also satisfies my criteria. The criteria are:

  • Consistent with my experience and self-consistent
  • Simple yet extensible to every facet of life
  • Actively leads me to a happier life

A non-exhaustive list of alternatives can be found here, along with relational analyses to the proposed system.

This work is an attempt to bridge that gap for myself. It is my hope that sharing it with others will generate feedback to accelerate its development, thereby helping all those who choose to be involved.

By posting it on a public repo I imply the following:

  • This is not the truth and it is not final - it is a work in progress
  • This is meant to be shared, dissected, discussed and possibly scrapped in favor of a better option
